The classic of classic games: Rock, Paper, Scissors. Even if you don't know how to play, We'll teach you!

How To Play:

Player must choose 1 of 3 options:

    "r" for Rock
    "p" for Paper
    "s" for Scissors

Once player chooses one of the options, the computer will also randomly pick one of the options. The outcomes are as followed:

    Rock beats Scissors
    Paper beats Rock
    Scissors beat Paper
    If Player and Computer chooses the same choice, then it is a tie
